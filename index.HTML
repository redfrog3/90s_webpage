Weapons and Sodas

Sodas

Sodas, or in other words are power-ups. As you progress through the game and unlock more rooms, you will find these different colored soda machines -(example above)-. There are a total of six sodas in the game, and these may be one of the most important aspects of the game.


-Soda type- -Function-
-Quick Revive- -Allows for faster revives of teammates or for self-revive in solo play-
-Juggernog- -Increases the number of hits a player can take before going down-
-Speed Cola- -Increases reload speed and barrier repair speed-
-Double Tap Root Beer- -Increases the fire rate of weapons-
-Stamin-Up -Increases movement speed-
-PhD Flopper- -Allows the player to survive and explode after falling from a high height-

Weapons

In Black Ops 1 Zombies, weapons fall into a few main groups. Wall weapons like the M14, Olympia, MP5K, AK74u, and Stakeout are cheap and reliable early on but lose power later unless Pack-a-Punched. The Mystery Box offers stronger but random guns such as the Galil, Commando, FAMAS, RPK, HK21, and weaker pulls like the Spectre, China Lake, or RPG. Some of the strongest options are the Wonder Weapons, including the Ray Gun and Thunder Gun on Kino and Ascension.


Pack-a-Punch - - - a machine that lets you upgrade a weapon, greatly boosting its damage, ammo capacity, and sometimes giving it new attachments or effects.
